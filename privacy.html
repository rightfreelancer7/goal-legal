GOAL – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-24
Contact: rightfreelancer7@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how the GOAL mobile application (“GOAL”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles information. GOAL is not intended for children under 13.

1) Account Registration

GOAL does not require you to create an account.

2) Data You Enter

You may enter goal titles, notes, schedules, and task details (“User Content”). By default, this data is stored locally on your device.

3) AI Processing (OpenAI)

When you request AI-generated plans or suggestions, GOAL sends relevant User Content you provide to the OpenAI API to generate a response. GOAL does not maintain a separate user database.

OpenAI’s handling of data is governed by OpenAI’s policies. We recommend reviewing OpenAI’s privacy information.

4) Advertising

GOAL displays ads using third-party advertising providers (for example, Google AdMob). These providers may collect and process device information such as advertising identifiers, approximate location, and basic app interaction signals to deliver and measure ads.

We configure ads to be professional and to avoid adult/sexually explicit ad content (for example, using “Teen”/“General” content ratings and related ad controls where available). However, ad networks ultimately control ad inventory.

5) Analytics

GOAL may use basic analytics to understand app performance (for example, crash logs and aggregated usage). If enabled, analytics providers may collect limited device and app activity information.

6) Data Sharing

We may share information only as needed to operate the app, including:

  • OpenAI (to generate AI responses you request)
  • Ad providers (to display and measure ads)
  • Service providers (hosting/logging) as needed to keep the app running

7) Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards and transmit data over encrypted connections (HTTPS) when communicating with external services. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Data Retention & Deletion

Since GOAL stores your goals locally on your device by default, uninstalling the app typically removes locally stored data. Third-party services (ads/AI) may retain data according to their policies.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
